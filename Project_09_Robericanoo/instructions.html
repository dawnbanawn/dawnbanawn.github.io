ROBERICANO the ultimate schedule if you only have one game court.



Web-based schedule. Works on all devices.


This app provides you with a designed schedule for different sports, which also keeps track of scores, game time, and break time. You can see the total score, upcoming matches, and also edit results from previous matches. The schedule is made to provide a balanced line-up between all players, so that everyone gets to play with and against everyone. This schedule promotes shorter matches with breaks included, for fast, energetic and tough duels.


This app schedule can be used in most sports where you play doubles. For example: Padel, Tennis, Table Tennis, Beach Volleyball, Badminton, Pickleball, Street Basketball and more, only your imagination sets the limits. It's made to be simple enough to be used by friends and family who just want to compete in a social way, and professional enough to be used by sports venues.


Suitable playing time for this schedule is 2 hours, which means that the breaks are not too long and the matches are not too short, but the total playing time can be shortened if you want. Absolutely the best AW activity and of course everyday gaming.


At present, the schedule supports 1 course with 4-6 players. New schedules and updates are likely to come in the future.
